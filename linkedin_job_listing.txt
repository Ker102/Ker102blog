## 📝 Job Description (MDX/Plain Text)

**Role**: Founding AI Engineer  
**Company**: Kaelux (kaelux.dev)  
**Location**: Remote / Estonia-friendly  
**Focus**: LLM Orchestration, Autonomous Agents (LangGraph), and Systems Integration  

### **About the Role**
Kaelux is a global AI Engineering & Systems Integration Agency bridging the gap between technical innovation and production-ready infrastructure. We don’t just "use AI"—we engineer it into the core of the enterprise.

We are looking for our first **Founding AI Engineer**—someone who can take our deep technical research and turn it into high-scale, reliable intelligent systems for our global partners.

### **What You’ll Be Doing**
*   **Intelligent Orchestration**: Designing and deploying autonomous workflow agents using LangGraph and n8n.
*   **Advanced RAG Systems**: Engineering production-grade Retrieval-Augmented Generation (CRAG) anchored in proprietary data.
*   **Infrastructure Management**: Handling the lifecycle, monitoring, and security of AI agents in production (OpenClaw Cloud).
*   **Collaboration**: Working directly with the founder to shape the engineering roadmap of the agency and its flagship products (**ViperMesh** and **PromptTriage**).

### **Who You Are**
*   **Deep Technical Literacy**: You understand the difference between "Naive RAG" and "Agentic RAG." You know what a model router is and why it matters.
*   **Engineering-First Mindset**: You prioritize reliability, security, and scalability over chat-interface hype. 
*   **Self-Driven**: You thrive at the intersection of LLM orchestration and systems architecture.

### **Why Join Kaelux?**
You will be the first engineering hire at an agency that is already building state-of-the-art AI. You’ll have a direct hand in shaping the technical identity of the next great European AI powerhouse.
