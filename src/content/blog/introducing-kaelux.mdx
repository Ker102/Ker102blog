Kaelux is not a consultancy. We are an **Engineering Partner**. We don't just provide advice; we design and deploy the intelligent systems that will define the next decade of business. Our goal is to ensure that AI isn't just an "add-on," but the core operating system of the modern enterprise.

## The Kaelux Ecosystem: Products & Services

Over the past year, we have been building the foundational technologies that now power our agency's core offerings. These aren't just "tools"—they are production-grade platforms.

### 🕸️ ViperMesh: The Intelligent Orchestration Layer
ViperMesh is our flagship platform for intelligent mesh networking. In an era of distributed AI, ViperMesh provides the high-availability orchestration layer required for autonomous agents to communicate, plan, and execute across complex infrastructure.

### 🧪 PromptTriage: The RAG & Prompt Hygiene Standard
Data is the fuel, but retrieval is the engine. PromptTriage is our ecosystem for advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation). By implementing a **6-level RAG taxonomy** and **Corrective RAG (CRAG)** architectures with Brave Search fallback, we ensure zero-hallucination knowledge retrieval for enterprise workflows.

### ☁️ OpenClaw Cloud: Managed Agent Hosting
Building an agent is easy. Keeping it running at scale is not. **OpenClaw** is our managed hosting service that provides the lifecycle management, monitoring, and security required for AI agents to operate in production with 99.9% uptime.
