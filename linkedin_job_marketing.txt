## 📝 Job Description (MDX/Plain Text)

**Role**: Growth & Brand Lead  
**Company**: Kaelux (kaelux.dev)  
**Location**: Remote / Estonia-friendly  
**Focus**: Technical SEO, Content Strategy, and Brand Authority  

### **About the Role**
Kaelux is a global AI Engineering & Systems Integration Agency building high-performance autonomous infrastructure. We don’t just "use AI"—we engineer it into the core of the enterprise.

To support our technical leadership, we are looking for a **Growth & Brand Lead** who can translate our deep technical research into a high-authority brand presence across the global market. You will own the content distribution engine that powers our Engineering Hub (`engineering.kaelux.dev`) and our social presence.

### **What You’ll Be Doing**
*   **Authority Distribution**: Managing the "Kaelux Content Engine"—syndicating engineering research across Hashnode, LinkedIn, and specialized developer platforms.
*   **Technical SEO**: Optimizing our visibility as a "Digitally-native AI Engineering Agency" and bridging the gap between our research and client demand.
*   **Brand Narrative**: Scaling the "Future-Proofing" and "Engineering-First" story of the agency.
*   **Collaboration**: Working directly with the founder to define the growth strategy for our flagship products (**ViperMesh** and **PromptTriage**).

### **Who You Are**
*   **Technically Literate**: You don't need to write code, but you must understand terms like "RAG," "Orchestration," and "System Integration." You can talk to engineers.
*   **Data-Driven**: You understand how to turn obscure research metrics into compelling market narratives.
*   **Self-Driven**: You thrive at the intersection of B2B lead generation and high-tech brand authority.

### **Why Join Kaelux?**
You will be the first non-engineering hire at an agency that is already building state-of-the-art AI. You’ll have a direct hand in shaping the identity of a high-growth AI powerhouse.
